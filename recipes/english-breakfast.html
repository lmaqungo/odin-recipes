English breakfast

Description

Full English breakfast is a traditional British breakfast meal, consisting of several fried foods, usually served with a variety of side dishes and beverages. The main ingredients are bacon, eggs, sausages, tomatoes, mushrooms, beans, and toast.

Ingredients

  • 4 bacon slices
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 sausages
  • 1 tomato
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of baked beans
  • 2 slices of toast

Instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and cook the bacon slices until crispy.
  2. Remove the bacon from the pan and set aside. In the same pan, cook the sausages until browned and cooked through.
  3. Remove the sausages from the pan and set aside. Crack the eggs into the pan and cook until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  4. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside. Add the tomato and mushrooms to the pan and cook until softened.
  5. Heat the baked beans in a small saucepan over medium heat until heated through.
  6. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the full English breakfast by placing the bacon, sausages, eggs, tomato, mushrooms, and baked beans on a plate. Serve with the toast on the side.
